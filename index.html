About Us

A program built on sacred intention.

Jain Sanskar Arohan (JSA) is a Spiritual Educational Program launched in 2017 to help seekers of all ages understand Jain concepts, philosophies, values and way of life. This structured course offers a unique opportunity to learn and grow spiritually.

Why JSA?

Wisdom for a restless world.

  • Deepen your Spiritual Understanding – a true need of the hour.
  • Discover how Jain principles foster peace, balance and holistic living.
  • Explore the connection between Science and Jain Philosophy.
  • Inculcate values of non-violence, compassion, and truthfulness in everyday life.
